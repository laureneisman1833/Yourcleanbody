Homemade Almond Milk

Creamier than dairy and safer for little ones. Simple steps to make nutrient-rich almond milk at home.

Ingredients

1 cup raw organic almonds
4 cups filtered water
Pinch of sea salt

Instructions

Soak almonds 8-12 hrs. Drain, rinse, blend with water 60 sec. Strain through nut milk bag. Refrigerate up to 4 days.

Pro Tip

Save the pulp! Dry in a low oven and blitz into almond flour for baking.
