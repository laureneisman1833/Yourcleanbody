Our Story

Over the past 15 years, I have had the privilege of consulting with individuals and families, helping them make practical, sustainable changes that support lasting wellness.

Homemade Almond Milk

Prep: 10 min
Soak: 8-10 hrs
Yields: 4 cups

Ingredients

  • 2 cups raw almonds
  • Filtered water
  • 4 cups alkaline water
  • 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp Agave or Honey

Instructions

  1. Soak almonds 8-10 hrs, drain, rinse
  2. Blend with 4 cups alkaline water for 1 min
  3. Strain through nut milk bag
  4. Add salt, cinnamon, agave, blend again
