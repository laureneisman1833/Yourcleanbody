Homemade Almond Milk

Prep: 10 min
Soak: 8-10 hrs
Yields: 4 cups

Ingredients

  • 2 cups raw almonds
  • Filtered water for soaking
  • 4 cups filtered alkaline water
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp Agave or Honey

Instructions

  1. Soak 2 cups raw almonds in filtered water for 8-10 hours
  2. Drain and rinse almonds thoroughly
  3. Add to blender with 4 cups filtered alkaline water
  4. Blend well for a full minute
  5. Strain into a large bowl using a nut milk bag
  6. Return milk to a clean blender
  7. Add 1 tsp sea salt, 1 tsp cinnamon, and 1 tbsp Agave or Honey
  8. Blend again briefly — and enjoy!
