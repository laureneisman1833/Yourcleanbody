Homemade Almond Milk

Making your own almond milk eliminates hidden toxins from your diet. Store-bought often contains carrageenan, gums, and preservatives.

Prep: 10 min
Soak: 8+ hrs
Yields: 4 cups

Ingredients: 1 cup raw organic almonds, 4 cups filtered water, pinch sea salt.

Instructions: Soak almonds 8-12 hrs. Drain, rinse, blend with water 60-90 sec. Strain through nut milk bag. Refrigerate 3-4 days.

Pro tip: Save the pulp! Dry and blitz into almond flour.

My Story

After decades of struggling with acne, headaches, and endometriosis, I discovered something powerful: while I couldn't control every challenge my body faced, I could take control of how I cared for it.

For me, diet and lifestyle became more than a way to manage symptoms — they became a path to reclaiming my well-being. When we nourish our bodies and prioritize our health, we give ourselves the best opportunity to feel our strongest, pursue our goals, and live life with confidence and purpose.
